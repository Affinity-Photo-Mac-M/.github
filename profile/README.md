## Overview

Affinity Photo Advanced is a professional photo editing and RAW processing application that delivers comprehensive non-destructive editing, compositing, and retouching capabilities at a one-time purchase price that makes professional-grade image editing accessible without subscription overhead.

RAW processing handles files from all major camera manufacturers with exposure correction, white balance, noise reduction, and lens correction tools that extract the maximum quality from any camera's raw sensor data before entering the editing workflow.

Live filters and adjustment layers apply effects non-destructively as editable stack components that can be modified, reordered, or removed at any time, maintaining full creative flexibility throughout the editing process without committing to irreversible changes.

PSD compatibility ensures that Photoshop files open and save correctly with layers, blending modes, and adjustment layers preserved, making Affinity Photo Advanced a practical alternative for users working in mixed-tool environments.

For photographers and digital artists who need professional image editing depth without the recurring subscription cost of market-leading alternatives, Affinity Photo Advanced provides the capability that professional work demands at a price that respects the user's investment.

## Performance

Affinity Photo balances performance with system resource usage intelligently. Operations are handled in the background, keeping your Mac responsive for everything else. Efficient memory management and smart caching reduce overhead during extended work sessions.

Apple Silicon acceleration is fully utilized on M-series Macs, delivering noticeably faster performance. On Intel machines, hardware acceleration is used wherever available.
